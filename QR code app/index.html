why animation is needed

Animation is important because it makes us be able to tell stories and communicate emotions and ideas in a unique, easy-to-perceive way that both small children and adults can understand.
Animation has helped connect people throughout the world in a way that sometimes writing and live-action films cannot.
One of the biggest benefits of animation is the ability to describe complex ideas in a simple way. For example, technology companies often use animation to convey a how complex solution or process works. It's the perfect solution for breaking things down that aren't easily explained or even possible to film.
